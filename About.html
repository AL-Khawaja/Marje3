About us

Our vision is to support the student from all academic and advisory aspects, promote the values ​​of cooperation and teamwork, support skills, build an educational life and social experience, help students to communicate and to discuss.

Club values:
Excellence in performance, teamwork, affiliation, and responsibility.

 Objectives:
- Encouraging volunteer tendencies, serving students, promoting the concept of teamwork, and consolidating the values ​​of cooperation among students.
- Supporting academic majors with a practical aspect to raise students’ technical efficiency and help them.
- Follow up students who fail academically.
- Breaking the barrier of fear among students and getting to know new colleagues.
- Create a database.
- Finally, returned something of the kindness that our dear university offered us.

 The club manages, plans and organizes activities and programs, also provides academic and advisory services through the following:
- Forming a volunteer team to serve the university and students.
- Team of lectures and material references (year questions, books, summaries, notebooks).
- Social media and design team.
- Receiving new students and introducing them to the university and its majors.
- Guiding and assisting students (in selecting subjects before the start of registration in each semester).
- Holding revision classes before the exams period by students who excel in the subject.

 Supporting bodies:
- University students.
- Learning support office.
- Employees in various departments and deanships.
- Club heads.
- Social Counselor.
- Consultation and training center.


 Additional ideas:
- Gathering Erasmus students and cultural exchange to talk about their experiences and help students in their application + making an explanatory video.
- Organizing trips to companies (according to specialization).
- Photographing university life and highlighting it in the best way.
